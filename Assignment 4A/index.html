FAQ

How do I reset my password?

Go to Settings → Account Details → Change Password. Enter your current password and choose a new one that meets the security requirements.

How do I invite team members?

Navigate to Settings → Team → Invite Members. Enter their email addresses and select appropriate roles. They will receive an invitation link within minutes.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings → Export Data. You can download your data in CSV, JSON, or PDF format. Exports are processed within 24 hours.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency. All payments are encrypted with 256-bit SSL security.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with access to all Pro features. No credit card required. Cancel anytime with no questions asked.
